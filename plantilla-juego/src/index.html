How to play
Play as Dr Mariano in order to destroy all the viruses in the bottle. Move and rotate the pills, align four capsules or viruses of the same color to destroy them.
Compete against your friends in the 1 vs 1 mode, clear the board before your oponent three times to achieve a victory!
